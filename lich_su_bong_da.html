Introduction

Football is the world’s most popular sport, with ancient origins and a long process of development — from primitive ball-kicking games to professional systems and global tournaments such as the World Cup.

Important Milestones

  1. 2nd century BC – 2nd century AD: Early civilizations had primitive ball-kicking games.
  2. 19th century: Modern football was codified in England.
  3. 1904: FIFA was founded to govern world football.
  4. 1930: The first World Cup was held in Uruguay.
  5. 1970–present: Football became a global industry tied to media, economy, and popular culture.
